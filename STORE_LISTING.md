## Full Description (4000 chars max)
Can you find the hidden 4×4 pattern in 8 turns or fewer?

16 Cells is a minimalist logic puzzle grounded in information theory. Each turn, you guess a black-and-white grid pattern, and the game tells you how many cells match. Use deduction to narrow down the possibilities — and track your progress in bits of information gained.

HOW IT WORKS
• You're given a hint: the number of white cells in the answer
• Submit a guess and receive the exact match count
• Each guess reveals information, measured in bits
• Once your total gained bits reaches the required amount, only one possibility remains

TWO MODES
• Endless — Infinite random puzzles. Build your streak.
• Daily — Same puzzle for everyone. Compare your score.

FEATURES
• Clean, distraction-free interface
• Real-time information theory stats (bits/turn, candidates remaining)
• Satisfying minimal sound design
• No ads. No accounts. Just pure logic.

Inspired by Shannon's information theory. Built for people who enjoy thinking.
