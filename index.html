How to play

Find the hidden 3×3 black & white pattern in 6 turns or fewer.

You start with a hint: the number of white cells in the answer. Tap cells to toggle them, then submit your guess.

After each guess, you'll see how many cells match exactly between your pattern and the answer. Use this to narrow down the possibilities.

When 5 or fewer candidates remain, they'll be revealed — click one to set it as your next guess.

A new puzzle every day. The fewer turns you use, the better your score.

Tip: choose patterns that split the remaining possibilities as evenly as possible. That's maximum information per turn.
