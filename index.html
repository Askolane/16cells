How to play

Find the hidden 4×4 black & white pattern in 8 turns or fewer.

You start with a hint: the number of black cells in the answer. Tap cells to toggle them, then submit your guess.

After each guess, you'll see how many black cells you got right. Use this to narrow down the possibilities.

When 5 or fewer candidates remain, they'll be revealed — click one to set it as your next guess.

The information theory behind it

Each puzzle has a certain number of bits of information needed to identify the answer. For example, if there are 3,003 possible patterns, you need ~11.6 bits to narrow it down to one.

Every guess you make reveals some bits of information through the feedback. Once your total gained bits reaches the required amount, only one possibility remains — the answer is determined.

The progress bar below the grid tracks this: gained / required bits. Maximizing bits per turn is the key to solving in fewer guesses.

Endless mode: Infinite puzzles. Keep your streak alive.

Daily mode: Same puzzle for everyone. Compare scores.

Tip: choose patterns that split the remaining possibilities as evenly as possible — that yields the maximum bits per turn.
