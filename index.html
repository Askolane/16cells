How to play

Find the hidden 4×4 pattern in 8 turns or fewer.

Your only starting clue is how many cells make up the answer.

Submit a guess, and you'll be told how many cells match the answer.

Each guess earns 💡 Idea Points based on how useful it was. The higher, the better.

Once your total 💡 reaches the required amount, the answer is determined — only one possibility remains.

Go for a long streak! Good luck!
