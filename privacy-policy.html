Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 20, 2026

Overview

Halve ! is a puzzle game that respects your privacy. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

Data Storage

All game data — including your progress, scores, and preferences — is stored locally on your device using your browser's localStorage. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

Analytics & Tracking

We do not use any analytics services, tracking pixels, cookies, or third-party scripts that collect user data.

Third-Party Services

The app loads fonts from Google Fonts. Google's privacy policy applies to that service. No other third-party services are used.

Advertising

Halve ! does not display any advertisements.

Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes

If this policy changes, we will update this page with the new date.
